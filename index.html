About CREATIVE wing

Computer Researchers & Engineers Association for Technical Innovation & Versatile Education Wing (CREATIVE Wing) is a global private and autonomous non-profit academic consortium focused on innovation and research excellence. CREATIVE Wing network comprises several leading academicians and scientists from academia and industry across the globe. We primarily aim to provide a platform to motivate researchers and innovators to do their collaborative research in the field of computers, information technology and related domains. We are committed to be the leading independent academic and professional body working in collaboration with academicians, students, researchers, industry experts, NGOs, educational institutes and government bodies. This will motivate us in playing a pivotal role in society by disseminating teaching and research on a global scale, a focus on real life applications necessary for the welfare of mankind, and an ability to combine novelty with quality. We actively participate and promote R&D activities such as conferences, faculty development programs, workshops, seminars, summer / winter schools, research collaborations, patents, book publications and collaborative national / international projects in India as well as in other parts of the world.
